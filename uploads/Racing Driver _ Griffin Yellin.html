I’m from New York City. And I’m Just Getting Started.

MY
STORY

Born and raised in New York City, I am a recent graduate of the Dalton School. I found racing as a refuge from my grief over my father's passing in 2022. Since then, I have fully dedicated myself to racing, joining the Skip Barber Racing School in their formula race series.

 

I have competed in races at prestigious tracks such as Sebring International Raceway, Road America, Circuit of the Americas, and more. My ultimate goal now is to secure sponsorship to pursue my dream of racing in the esteemed NTT INDYCAR series and becoming the first Puerto Rican to do so.
